Haiku

A haiku is a short poem consisting of 3 lines with a 5-7-5 syllable structure. Originating in Japan, it often reflects nature or emotions.

Example Haikus

Gentle spring morning
petals drift on soft breezes
time stands still for now

Crimson leaves falling
whispers of the coming chill
earth prepares for sleep
